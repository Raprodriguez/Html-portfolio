TOP 3 BEST MOVIES TO RECOMMEND

by: Raph Rodriguez

SOUL

This is one of Disneys best animated movies in my opinion.
I can't even believe why this has not won any awards at all.
You will undoubtedly cry.

KIMI NO NA WA

When it comes to Japanese animated film, nothing comes close
to the movie "YOUR NAME". This movie has the best animation
of all time as well as the best plot twist in movie history,
and they have the awards to prove it!

MARLEY AND ME

One of the saddest movie about dogs. This movie will show you
how tough it is to own a dog that has a certain personality, and even
if it is hard, every hardship is all worth it. You will surely cry like a baby.
